Spite and Malice - How to Play

🎯 Objective

Be the first player to empty your Payoff pile (13 cards). The faster you clear it, the more points you score.

Basic Rules

  • Play cards to the 4 Center piles in ascending order (A → Q). Kings are wild.
  • You can play from your Hand, Payoff pile, or Side Stacks.
  • You may play as many cards as possible in one turn.
  • When you cannot or choose not to play anymore, discard **one** card from your hand to a Side Stack to end your turn.
  • If your hand reaches 0 cards during your turn, you immediately draw back up to 5 cards from the stock.

Scoring

When someone empties their Payoff pile:

  • +5 points for winning the hand
  • +1 point per card left in opponent's Payoff pile
  • Streak Bonus: Extra points for playing multiple cards in one turn (higher if from Payoff pile)

Strategies & Tips

  • Prioritize playing from your Payoff pile whenever possible.
  • Save Kings for critical moments or long chains.
  • Watch your opponent's Payoff top card and try to block them (Spite!).
  • Keep your Side Stacks organized — don't bury useful cards too deep.
  • Long streaks = big points. Try to chain 4+ cards when possible.

Installing as an App (Recommended)

On Android/Chrome: Tap the menu (⋮) → "Install app" or "Add to home screen".

On iPhone/Safari: Tap Share → "Add to Home Screen".

Once installed, the game runs in true fullscreen with no browser bars.

Best Viewing Experience

• Rotate your phone to Portrait mode for best layout.
• Use "Add to Home Screen" for fullscreen experience.
• Avoid zooming manually — the game is optimized for mobile.
